Mountain Bikes

What are mountain Bikes?

Mountain bikes are bikes that are used to offroad. They can be used on any terrain and designed to handle rough surfaces. Mountain bikes tend to be more durable and stronger than road bikes due to the more demanding conditions they are used in. The tires tend to be a little bigger and have more traction.


How much do they cost?

The cheapest mountain bike that Viscious Cycles offer is the gently owned starter mountain bikes that start at $200.00 dollars. New bikes start at around $300 dollars. If interested in a custom built mountain bike, please call us for an estimate as price varies based on design.
