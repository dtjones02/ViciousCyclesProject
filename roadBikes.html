Road Bikes

What are road bikes?

Road bikes are bikes that are used on flat paved surfaces. Road bikes are slimmer in design, light-weight, and have thin tires. These features help cyclist travel faster. Road bikes are used for sport, commuting around cities, and leisure activities.


How much do they cost?

Vicious Bikes offers gently used road bikes starting at $100 dollars. New road bikes start at about $250 dollars. If you are interested in a custom build, please contact us as prices vary based on design.
