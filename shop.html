How can I buy from Vicious Cyles?

Vicious Cycle products can be purchased/ordered at out store. Come down today and we'll get started with a purchase of an existing bike with cash, credit, or in-house loan approval.


What do we offer?

At Vicious Cycles we specialize in mountain bikes and road bikes. We are currently in the process of expanding our market to offer accessories as well. The Vicious Cycle team also has a passion for making custom build bikes. All custom builds will require a 25% down payment before projects will begin, and either payment in full, or an approved in-house loan, or a transfer of funds to a third-party vendor. All purchases on custom cycles are final.
